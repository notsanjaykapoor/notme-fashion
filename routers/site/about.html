Sustainable fashion encompasses the creation and consumption of clothing in a way that minimizes environmental and social impacts, promoting ethical practices throughout the supply chain. It aims for a fashion industry that is environmentally responsible, socially just, and economically viable. This includes using sustainable materials, ethical production practices, and promoting circularity through recycling, upcycling, and extending the lifespan of garments.

This site focuses on recycling, upcycling, and extending the lifespan of garments by providing an authentic environment for discovery, re-sale, borrowing, trading of used garments. It aims to reduce the friction that exists today, and relies heavily on individuals with integrity, character, and solid reputations.
